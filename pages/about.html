OUR STORY

A kitchen born from fire, built on craft, and driven by obsession with extraordinary food.

FIRE — The Manifesto

We Cook with
Radical Conviction

VOLCANICO was founded in 2018 by Chef Aryan Kapoor after returning from a decade abroad, cooking in Michelin-starred kitchens in Tokyo, Paris, and Copenhagen. He returned with one obsession: to recreate the primal satisfaction of food cooked over an open flame — elevated to fine dining standards.

Every dish at VOLCANICO starts with fire. Not as a technique, but as a philosophy. We believe that char, smoke, and sear unlock flavour compounds no other method can replicate. Our volcanic stone grill reaches 950°C. Our smoke house uses six different woods. Our kitchen team of 14 has an average of 8 years international experience.

— What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔥
Fire First

Every dish begins with fire as its central principle. We choose techniques that honour the primal nature of cooking with flame.

🌱
Seasonal & Local

Our menu changes with the seasons. We source from within 200km where possible, and build relationships with farmers who share our values.

🎯
Radical Precision

We obsess over temperature, timing, and texture. Every dish is tested hundreds of times before it earns a place on our menu.

🤝
People & Planet

We pay living wages, compost 95% of our kitchen waste, and run on 100% renewable energy. Good food must come with good conscience.

— Our Journey

The History of the Flame

2018

The Spark

VOLCANICO opens its first 40-seat restaurant in Bandra West. Fully booked within 3 days of opening. Zomato rates it 4.8 in the first month.

2019

First Flame Award

Chef Aryan wins Best New Restaurant at The Times Food & Nightlife Awards. The volcanic lamb rack becomes Mumbai's most talked-about dish.

2020

Pivoting to Delivery

During the pandemic, VOLCANICO launched premium delivery boxes. 3,000 orders in the first week. Featured in Vogue India, GQ, and The Hindu's food section.

2022

Expansion

We expand the restaurant to 120 seats and add a private dining room and bar. Chef Meera joins the team, transforming our dessert menu.

2025

Today

12 awards, 50,000+ guests, and still the same obsession with fire. We're now building our second kitchen, coming 2026.
